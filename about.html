About this app

iTechAssist is built for field technicians and engineers who need fast, accurate fault diagnosis on the job.

Using AI, the app analyses fault descriptions and images submitted by technicians and returns step-by-step troubleshooting guidance in real time.

Who it is built for

  • Field technicians handling installations and repairs
  • Biomedical engineers maintaining medical equipment
  • Software and control engineer
  • Structural and civil engineers
  • Electrical and mechanical engineer
  • HVAC and instrumentation specialists
  • site supervisors managing multiple fault reports
