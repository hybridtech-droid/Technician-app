iTechAssist - fault diagnosis for Technical service personnel

What this app does

This app helps field technicians log equipment faults and get AI-powered diagnosis in real time.

Technicians can input symptoms and the app will analyse the data to suggest possible causes and solutions.

Get started by logging a new faults or viewing existing ones.

It also provides direct links to spare parts and component dealers.

use the app to quickly identify and resolve equipment issues, improving efficiency and reducing downtime.

Engineering fields covered

  • Biomedical engineering
  • Electrical engineering
  • Mechanical engineering
  • Electronics and instrumentation
  • HVAC and refrigeration

Faulty severity levels

  1. Critical - equipment down, immediate response needed
  2. High - degraded performance, fix within 4 hours
  3. Medium - partial fault, fix within 24 hours
  4. Low - minor issue, fix at next schedule maintenance
